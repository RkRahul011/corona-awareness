What is coronavirus?

Corona viruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS).

It is the largest category for an RNA virus. In most cases, it is difficult to detect if coronavirus or a different cold-causing virus is causing you fever. Known as “COVID-19”, the newly identified coronavirus can cause pneumonia, and like other respiratory infections, it has an incubation of between 1 and 14 days.COVID-19 can be severe, and has caused millions of deaths around the world as well as lasting health problems in some who have survived the illness. The coronavirus can be spread from person to person. It is diagnosed with a test.

how to prevent?

wear a mask

may reduce the spread of coronavirus droplets in certain circumstances.

wash your hand

Handwashing with soap removes germs from hands.

consult doctor

Doctor can understand your problem and give you medicines for relief.

avoid touching

Avoid touching eyes, nose and mouth.

avoid contact

Hands touch many surfaces and can pick up viruses.

clean everyday

Cleaning and disinfecting high-touch surfaces in your home regularly is an important precaution to lower the risk of infection.
